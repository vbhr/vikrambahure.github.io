Vikram Bahure

I am a Post-Doctoral Researcher at the Department of International Development at King's College London. I am part of economic intervention team in theALIVE project which focuses on improving adolescent mental health by tackling the impact of poverty.

My primary research interest is development economics and applied economics. I specialize in marriage markets, political economy, and internal migration. I use causal empirical designs to study questions on early marriage, crime against women, voter preferences, politician characteristics, and migrant household wellbeing.

I obtained my Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Geneva.

Research interest (primary): Development Economics and Applied Economics

Secondary interest: adolescent mental health, political economy, marriage markets, and internal migration
